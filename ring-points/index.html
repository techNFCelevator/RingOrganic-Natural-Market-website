Ring for great rewards!

Ring Points are an easy way to get the best value from your groceries! Here's how the points system works:

  • You earn 10 Ring Points for every $1 you spend.
  • Points redemptions are by transaction value.
  • You can start redeeming points as soon as you collected at least 1000 points.
  • Points do not expire. You can earn as many as you like on any phone number you choose to use! Anyone can earn points on any chosen phone number.
  • Redeeming points requires photo ID that matches the accountholder's phone number.
  • Each reward account is assigned one phone number. Points are transferrable.

If you are currently not a Ring Points member, here's how you can register:

  1. Shop normally at one of our stores in person.
  2. Provide the phone number you wish to use at checkout. If not asked, then please ask for Ring Points registration.
  3. Complete checkout transaction and you earned your first set of points!

Need to know your current account balance and transaction history? You can check online!
